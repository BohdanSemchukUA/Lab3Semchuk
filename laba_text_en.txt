In the modern world, the issue of nature conservation has become especially relevant.Human activity leaves a significant impact on the environment: from deforestation to pollution of reservoirs and atmosphere.Such activity gradually destroys ecosystems, threatens the disappearance of many species of animals and plants, as well as impairs the quality of life of humans.
However, nature is not only a source of resources, but also an important factor that affects our health and well -being.Clean air, pure water and wealth of natural landscapes give us harmony and peace, promote physical and psychological health.
